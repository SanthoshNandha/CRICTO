Step 1: Click on two terms to select and click GENERATE.
Step 2: Provide the relation between the two terms and the relavant date in the pop up.
Step 3: The links will be visible in the Visualization view
Step 4: Click 'GET RELATED DOCUMENTS' to get documents related to the links created.
Step 5: Create relations across the related documents by clicking on the terms
Step 6: You can note important terms in scratch pad and create links from them.
Step 7: You can edit the created relations in the edit view, by selecting 'Edit'
Step 8: Click 'FINISH' button to get Survey code.
